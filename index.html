    May the schwa be with you!

    What is schwa in English grammar? Maybe you already know? If you want to sound like a native English speaker you must be aware of this pronunciation known as a schwa. The 5 vowel sounds, a e i o u, can get this sound. The sound is close to the sound of "u" in the word umbrella.

    examples:

    • America = Umerucu
    • Canada = Canudu
    • Japan = Jupan
    • New Zealand = New Zealund
